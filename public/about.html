About BERT Model

What is BERT?

BERT (Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers) is a transformer-based machine learning technique for natural language processing (NLP) developed by Google.

This demo shows how BERT can understand the context of words in a sentence, making it powerful for tasks like sentiment analysis, question answering, and more.

How It Works

  1. Enter your text in the input field
  2. Our system processes it using BERT
  3. Get instant analysis of your text
